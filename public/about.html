About Us

This web application was fully developed by a group of five University of Maryland Information Science students with the goal to bring gamers exactly what they have been looking for: a constantly expanding database of video games to help them decide what to play next!

We aim to provide gamers with the best information possible to make this decision, so each game within our database is equipped with a large variety of information such as the free-to-play capabilities, the player population and more!

If gamers are stuck between a few of the great options available within our database then they can use the graph feature of our web application to compare prices and see what game fits their budget best!
